Membership Information



We have 3 different types of membership:


Monthly Membership without Commitment : $0.00 Registration Fee, $59.00 Annual Fee, $31.00 1st Month ($29.57 per month + $1.13 Sale tax (4.85%))

The member shall pay $31 (tax included) per month. Said amount must be paid by Electronic Funds Transfer through either a checking account or a credit card. An additional annual membership fee of %59.00 will be billed every year (date will be based on the date when membership is activated). Dues and Annual Fees should not increase for a period of at least 12 months from the date this agreement is signed. A $10.00 service charge will be imposed for bounced or late payments.


The member has the right to cancel the membership at any time after the first two months of membership. Afterward, cancellation may be done with a 15 days prior written notification and surrender of the membership card. Any unpaid and/or pending dues must be paid to the club upon cancellation. Cancellation must be submitted in writing and in person.

Monthly Membership with 12 months Commitment : $0.00 Registration Fee, $59.00 Annual Fee, $21.00 1st Month ($19.99 per month + $0.97 Sale tax (4.85%))

The member shall pay $21 (tax included) per month. Said amount must be paid by Electronic Funds Transfer through either a checking account or a credit card. An additional annual membership fee of %59.00 will be billed every year (date will be based on the date when membership is activated). Dues and Annual Fees should not increase for a period of at least 12 months from the date this agreement is signed. A $10.00 service charge will be imposed for bounced or late payments.


Cancellation may be done at any time after 12 months has been satisfied with a 15 days prior written notification and surrender of the membership card. Any unpaid and/or pending dues must be paid to the club upon cancellation. Cancellation must be submitted in writing and in person.

Year Paid in Full : $313.00 ($298.53+4.85% tax)

The above mentioned onetime fee shall pay for 12 months of full membership. If the member renews prior to this renewal date, the member shall pay $298.53 plus 4.85% tax for a total of $313.00 for 12 months if membership, as a guarenteed renewal rate.


However, if the member does not renew by this renewal date, member shall pay the current new member fees in existence.
